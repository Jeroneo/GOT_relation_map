Harlan Grandison was a knight from House Grandison. He was a member of the Kingsguard during the reign of King Aerys II Targaryen.

History

Ser Harlan was a member of the Kingsguard under Aerys II and presumably his father Jaehaerys II before him, as he was an old man when Aerys reigned. He died in his sleep in his cell in the White Sword Tower of the Red Keep. Harlan's slot in the white cloaks was taken by a young Ser Jaime Lannister.[1][2]
