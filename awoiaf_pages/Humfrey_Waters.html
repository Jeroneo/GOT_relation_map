Humfrey Waters

Humfrey Waters

  • Commander of the City Watch
Allegiance City Watch of King's Landing
Culture Crownlands
Book A Feast for Crows (mentioned)

Humfrey Waters is a member of the City Watch of King's Landing and the captain of the Dragon Gate.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

After the imprisonment of Queen Cersei Lannister by the Faith, the small council removes Ser Osfryd Kettleblack as Commander of the City Watch, and promotes Humfrey to the position.[1]
