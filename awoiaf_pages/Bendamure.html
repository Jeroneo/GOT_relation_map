Bendamure

Bendamure
Title Maester
Allegiance The Citadel
Book Fire and Blood (mentioned)

Bendamure was a maester and the author of Six Times to Sea: Being an Account of the Great Voyages of Alyn Oakenfist.[1]

He may or may not have been the maester at Driftmark during Lord Alyn Velaryon's time.
