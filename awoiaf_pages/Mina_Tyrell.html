Mina Tyrell is a noblewoman of House Tyrell, the eldest daughter of Lord Luthor Tyrell and Lady Olenna Redwyne. She has two siblings: Mace Tyrell, the current Lord of Highgarden, and a younger sister, Janna Tyrell.[1]

Character

Mina is younger than her husband.[3]

History

Mina married her first cousin, Lord Paxter Redwyne of the Arbor. Together, they have three children, Ser Horas, Ser Hobber, and Desmera Redwyne.[1]
