Wendell Webber was a knight of House Webber during the reign of King Aerys I Targaryen. He was the cousin of Wyman Webber, Lord of Coldmoat. Wendell was married to the sister of Lord Rowan of Goldengrove. He and his wife had many sons and daughters.[2]

Appearance and Character

Wendell was a short, bald man with a goiter on his neck. He had a problem with flatulence.[2]

According to Septon Sefton, Wendell was grasping and stupid.[2]

History

Lord Wyman Webber dictated in his will that his daughter, Lady Rohanne Webber, must have married before the second anniversary of his death or Coldmoat and its lands would go to his cousin, Ser Wendell.[2]
