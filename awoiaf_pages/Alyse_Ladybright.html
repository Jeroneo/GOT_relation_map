Alyse Ladybright is a noblewoman of House Ladybright and the lord treasurer at Sunspear.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Prince Doran Martell left Lady Alyse in charge of the accounts of Sunspear when he departed for the Water Gardens.[2]
