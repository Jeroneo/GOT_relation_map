Lucas Codd, known as Left-Hand Lucas Codd, is an ironborn raider and member of House Codd.

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Left-Hand Lucas joins with Euron Greyjoy when Euron declares himself King of the Isles and the North.[3] Lucas and Asha Greyjoy trade insults at the kingsmoot on Old Wyk,[4] and he takes the dragon horn from Cragorn after the horn is blown.[5]

While feasting in the hall of the castle overlooking Lord Hewett's Town, Left-Hand Lucas tears tapestries down from the walls and wears one when Victarion Greyjoy enters the hall. After the feast, Lucas rapes one of Lord Humfrey Hewett's daughters.[6]

A Dance with Dragons

After learning she has been wed by proxy to Erik Ironmaker, Asha thinks Erik is at least better than Lucas or the Red Oarsman.[7]

The Winds of Winter

 Warning
This information has thus far been released in a sample chapter for The Winds of Winter, and might therefore not be in finalized form. Keep in mind that the content as described below is still subject to change.

Lucas feasts in the ruins of a castle near the Arbor with some of Euron's men, and he takes part in mocking Aeron Greyjoy when the latter is brought up from the hold of the Silence.[8]

Quotes

Lucas: I am unwed.

Asha: And for good reason. All women do despise the Codds as well. Don't look at me so mournful, Lucas. You still have your famous hand.
Euron: Was that courteous, Asha? You have wounded Lucas to the quick.

Asha: Easier than wounding him in the prick. I throw an axe as well as any man, but when the target is so small ...[4]

—Lucas, Asha Greyjoy, and Euron Greyjoy
