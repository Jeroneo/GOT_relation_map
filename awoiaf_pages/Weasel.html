Weasel, also known as the crying girl,[3][1] is a girl from the riverlands found by Yoren on his march north from King's Landing. Her real name is unknown.

Appearance and Character

No older than two,[3] Weasel is a young child with matted hair.[4] Lommy Greenhands gives her the name because he thinks she looks like the animal.[4]

Weasel often cries, and she never speaks a word after she is rescued. She is stubborn and resilient, but has a bad habit of eating mud.[4]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

While Yoren and his band of Night's Watch recruits travel north from King's Landing, they come across a village and holdfast which have been raided during the War of the Five Kings. Yoren, Murch, and Cutjack investigate the ruins and emerge with a little girl and a one-armed woman who dies soon after.[3] Arya Stark hopes that Shella Whent, Lady of Harrenhal, might be able to care for the child.[1]

At an unnamed Gods Eye town, the girl eagerly eats goose given to her by Hot Pie.[1] The settlement comes under attack by Ser Amory Lorch and his band of westermen, and Arya and Gendry help the traumatized girl through a secret tunnel to escape the fight at the holdfast.[1]

The crying girl stops whimpering, so Lommy Greenhands starts calling her Weasel because of her appearance.[4] The girl is unafraid of eating bugs, and she kicks Hot Pie.[4] When the youths are discovered by the Mountain's men, Weasel hears the soldiers coming and runs off into the woods. She is not found again.[4]

While imprisoned and forced to serve at Harrenhal by House Lannister, Arya takes the name "Weasel" for her own.[5]

A Feast for Crows

At the House of Black and White in Braavos, the big eyes and hollow face of the waif remind Arya of Weasel.[6]
