Shella

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search

Lady
Shella
Title Lady
Culture Westerosi
Book A Game of Thrones (mentioned)

Lady Shella is the focus of a famous love story with the Rainbow Knight.[1]

Recent Events

A Game of Thrones

Following the arrest of her father, Lord Eddard Stark, Sansa Stark is returned to her room in the Red Keep after promising to support King Joffrey I Baratheon. She loses herself in a collection of stories, one of which is the story of Lady Shella and the Rainbow Knight.[1]
