Clement Celtigar was the Lord of Claw Isle and the head of House Celtigar during the late part of the Dance of the Dragons. He was the son and heir of Lord Bartimos Celtigar.[1]

History

Clement became the Lord of Claw Isle after the death of his father during the riot in King's Landing in 130 AC.[2]

After the ascension of King Aegon III Targaryen, Lord Clement rode to King's Landing from Duskendale with Ser Corwyn Corbray and Lady Staunton.[1]
