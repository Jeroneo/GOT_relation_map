Spare Boot is a builder of the Night's Watch.[2]

Appearance

Spare Boot only has one leg, and his other leg is made of carved wood.[3]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Spare Boot is one of the men that Bowen Marsh leaves behind to defend Castle Black.[3] He uses scorpions from atop the Wall during the battle beneath the Wall,[4] and a wildling's arrow strikes his wooden leg.[5] Spare Boot congratulates Jon Snow after he is chosen Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.[6]

Quotes

The bloody buggers got my leg. The wooden one![5]

—Spare Boot to Jon Snow
