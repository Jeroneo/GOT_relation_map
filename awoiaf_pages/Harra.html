Character

Harra is an old, fierce woman.[1]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

After Lord Tywin Lannister takes over Harrenhal, Harra continues in service to the Lannisters.[1] When a captive Arya Stark arrives at Harrenhal, Harra and Amabel inspect her as they do the other captives, and eventually assign her to Weese.[1] After Lord Tywin departs Harrenhal and the castle falls to Lord Roose Bolton, Harra is executed for telling the servants of Lady Shella Whent to serve the Lannisters.[2]
