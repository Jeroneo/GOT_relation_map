Jeyne Lydden was a noblewoman from House Lydden. Her relation to Lewys Lydden, the Lord of the Deep Den, is unknown. She was the second wife of Ser Stevron Frey. They had two children, Aegon and Maegelle Frey.

History

Jeyne died of a fall from a horse.[2]
