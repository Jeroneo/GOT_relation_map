Kasporio, who styles himself Kasporio the Cunning,[2] is a bravo who serves as the second-in-command of the Second Sons, a sellsword company.[3]

Appearance and Character

Kasporio is slim and elegant, with a pointed beard. He wields a slender bravo's blade with a jeweled hilt, and wears a slashed pink doublet.[2]

The ruthless[1] Kasporio can be cruel and dismissive.[2]

Tyrion Lannister is annoyed by Kaspario, considering the bravo not so cunning[4] and a jackanapes.[2]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

Kasporio, along with the rest of the Second Sons, goes over to the Yunkai'i when Brown Ben Plumm thinks Daenerys Targaryen's cause is lost.[5][6]

When Tyrion Lannister writes out hundreds of documents promising large sums of gold to several high ranking members in the company, Kasporio is promised 10,000 golden dragons.[4] The bravo reports that three slave-catchers are searching the camps for the missing Tyrion and Penny.[4]

The Winds of Winter

 Warning
This information has thus far been released in a sample chapter for The Winds of Winter, and might therefore not be in finalized form. Keep in mind that the content as described below is still subject to change.

As the second siege of Meereen resumes, Kasporio goes with Brown Ben Plumm to visit Malazza, the Girl General. After they return he attends the meeting Ben has called. During the meeting he remarks that Malazza and Gorzhak zo Eraz, whom he calls "Pudding Face", are no better than one another.[7]
