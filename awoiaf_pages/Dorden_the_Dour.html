Ser Dorden the Dour is a knight in the service of King Stannis Baratheon. He is one of the queen's men.[1]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

Dorden stays with Queen Selyse at Eastwatch-by-the-Sea, when Stannis goes to take Deepwood Motte. When Selyse leaves Eastwatch to go to the Nightfort, he accompanies her to Castle Black.[2]
