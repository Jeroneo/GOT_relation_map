Gerrick Kingsblood's youngest daughter is a member of the newly fashioned House Redbeard.

Contents

  • 1 Appearance
  • 2 Recent Events
    • 2.1 A Dance with Dragons
  • 3 Family
  • 4 References

Appearance

Like her father and her ancestor, the Red Raven, she is kissed by fire. Jon Snow describes her hair as being of the same flame-red color as Ygritte's but hung long and straight. She is sweet looking and she is younger and more cowed than Val.[1]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

Gerrick and his family come through the Wall with the Free Folk following Tormund. The so-called "King of the Wildlings" presents his three daughters with him when he swears his oath to Jon Snow, who agrees to present them to Queen Selyse Florent.[1]

Selyse arranges the betrothal of Gerrick's youngest daughter to Ser Malegorn of Redpool, one of the queen's men and Queen Selyse's sworn sword.[2]
