Hazel Harte was a noblewoman from House Harte, during the reign of kings Viserys I, Aegon II, and Aegon III Targaryen.

History

Hazel was married to Daeron Velaryon. They had one child together, Lady Daenaera Velaryon, born in 127 AC.[2]

Hazel was one of the casualties of the Winter Fever. In 133 AC, her husband Daeron drowned in the Stepstones during the Daughters' War. Daenaera was left an orphan at the age of six, and was taken in as a ward by Lady Baela Targaryen and her husband, Lord Alyn Velaryon.[2]

Daenaera would become the queen consort of King Aegon III Targaryen,[2][3] and the mother of his five children.[4]
