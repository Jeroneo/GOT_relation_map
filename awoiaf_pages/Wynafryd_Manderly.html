Wynafryd Manderly is the eldest daughter of Ser Wylis Manderly and Leona Woolfield.[3]

Appearance

Wynafryd has brown hair bound in a long braid though shorter than her sister Wylla's.[4]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

As part of the terms of White Harbor being pardoned and returning to the king's peace of Tommen I Baratheon, Wynafryd is betrothed to the widowed Rhaegar Frey.[4]

When Lord Davos Seaworth visits the Merman's Court, Wylla proclaims that the Freys killed Lord Eddard, Lady Catelyn, and King Robb, and that House Manderly should join Stannis Baratheon in the name of vengeance. Wynafryd refers to her sister's exclamation as a child's foolishness.[4]

Wynafryd pretends to be pleased with her betrothal to Rhaegar. After faking the execution of Davos and releasing him from imprisonment in the Wolf's Den, her scheming grandfather, Lord Wyman Manderly, informs Davos he had already revealed his plans to Wynafryd and she knows the marriage will never take place.[5]

Wynafryd's betrothal ends with the suspected death of Rhaegar on his way to Winterfell.[6]

Quotes by Wynafryd

Quotes about Wynafryd

Not every man has it in him to be Prince Aemon the Dragonknight or Symeon Star-Eyes, and not every woman can be as brave as my Wylla and her sister Wynafryd... who did know, yet played her own part fearlessly.[5]

—Wyman Manderly to Davos Seaworth
