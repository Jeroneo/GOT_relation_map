Qarl, widely known as Qarl the Maid, is an ironborn warrior sworn to Balon Greyjoy.[2] He is the grandson of a thrall.[3]

Appearance and Character

See also: Images of Qarl

Qarl the Maid is beardless and pink-cheeked.[2][4] His straight sandy hair brushes against his shoulders.[3] He has a lean swimmer's physique,[3] a shy smile,[3] and is sarcastic.[4][3]

Qarl is renowned as one of the fiercest warriors of the Iron Islands.[5] He wields a longsword and dagger, and he is not an experienced horse rider.[3]

Qarl is Asha Greyjoy's favorite lover, which upsets Tristifer Botley.[3]

History

When Asha Greyjoy first met Qarl in 293 AC or 294 AC, she called his attempt at growing a beard "peach fuzz". Since he had never seen a peach, she encouraged him to join her crew. They traded at Fair Isle, Lannisport, and the Arbor,[3] and raided Lyseni pirates in the Stepstones.[6] Qarl and Asha began sleeping together after sampling peaches at the Arbor.[3]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

Qarl by cloudninja9©

Qarl and two companions, Skyte and Uller, encounter Theon and Asha Greyjoy in Lordsport. There they recount the story of the death of Skyte's brother, Eldiss. Asha tells Theon that he should make them part of his crew.[7]

A Feast for Crows

Qarl serves aboard Asha's ship, the Black Wind. He counts the longships gathered in support of her at Ten Towers and finds the number lacking.[8]

During the kingsmoot on Old Wyk, Qarl wins the finger dance against Eldred Codd, with one of Eldred's fingers landing in the wine cup of Ralf the Limper.[6] Qarl is one of the champions for Asha at the kingsmoot, but Euron Greyjoy is instead chosen King of the Isles and the North.[4]

A Dance with Dragons

Qarl sails to Deepwood Motte with Asha as she contemplates her next move after the kingsmoot. Qarl sleeps with Asha in Galbart Glover's bedchamber.[3]

Qarl wields his longsword in the fight by Deepwood Motte, and he kills a northman who grabs at Asha's hair. At one point he fights back to back with his lover. Qarl is captured along with Asha, Tristifer Botley, and several other ironborn by the army of Stannis Baratheon.[9]

Qarl is later freed and ransomed along with the other ironborn by Tycho Nestoris, as the Braavosi banker needs an escort to get him to Stannis whose army is marching on Winterfell.[10]

The Winds of Winter

 Warning
This information has thus far been released in a sample chapter for The Winds of Winter, and might therefore not be in finalized form. Keep in mind that the content as described below is still subject to change.

Qarl and Tristifer accompany Asha to her meeting with Stannis, when she asks about the fate of Theon, her captive brother. Theon considers Qarl and Tris to be Asha's pets.[11]
