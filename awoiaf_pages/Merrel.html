Merrel is a guard of House Baratheon of Dragonstone and a queen's man.

Character

Merrel is a hopeless drunkard but can be formidable fighter when not.[1]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

When Stannis Baratheon marches to Deepwood Motte Merrel is left at Castle Black to serve as one of Melisandre's guards.[1]
