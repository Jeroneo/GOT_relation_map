Donnor Stark was a Lord of Winterfell and head of House Stark. He was the eldest son of Lord Beron Stark and his wife, Lady Lorra Royce. He had four younger brothers (Willam, Artos, Errold and Rodrik) and two younger sisters (Berena and Alysanne).

History

According to a semi-canon source, Donnor's father, Lord Beron, was mortally wounded by ironborn, and House Stark faced a crisis of succession as his wife and four other recent Stark widows made claim for their own children.[3][4] In the end, Donnor succeeded his father as Lord of Winterfell.[5]

As Donnor died without issue, his brother Willam inherited Winterfell after Donnor's death.[5]

Donnor is buried in the crypts of Winterfell.[1]
