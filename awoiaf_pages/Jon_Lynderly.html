Jon Lynderly is the Lord of the Snakewood and the head of House Lynderly.[1]

Recent Events

A Feast for Crows

Petyr Baelish asks Lord Jon for a son to ward at the Eyrie with Lord Robert Arryn.[2] Jon sends his heir, Terrance Lynderly.[3][1]

Jon attends the wedding of Lord Lyonel Corbray.[3]
