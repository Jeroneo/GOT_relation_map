Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Ser Dudley was slain in the riverlands during the War of the Five Kings by House Lannister forces. When Sandor Clegane was captured by the brotherhood without banners and put on trial for crimes committed by his fellow Lannister forces, Ser Dudley was among the long list of Lannister victims named by Tom of Sevenstreams whose murders Clegane was to stand trial for.[1]

Quotes

Blind Wyl the Whittler. Goodwife Maerie. Maerie the Whore. Becca the Baker. Ser Raymun Darry, Lord Darry, young Lord Darry. The Bastard of Bracken. Fletcher Will. Harsley. Goodwife Nolla—

Beric: People. People great and small, young and old. Good people and bad people, who died on the points of Lannister spears or saw their bellies opened by Lannister swords.[1]

—Tom of Sevenstreams, Sandor Clegane, and Beric Dondarrion
