Dryn

Dryn is a son of Tormund Giantsbane.[2] He is about the same age as Bran Stark.[1]

Appearance

Dryn is a chunky boy, with short legs, thick arms, a wide red face, and a shock of dark brown hair. He looks like a miniature version of his father.[1]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

When Longspear Ryk comes to 'steal' Munda, Dormund and Dryn give Ryk a fight to defend their sister's honor.[3]

A Dance with Dragons

Tormund and over three thousand free folk declare a truce with the Night's Watch. Dryn is with his father Tormund, his brother Toregg, and Jon Snow as the wildlings pass the Wall at Castle Black. Tormund gives Dryn to Jon as a hostage. Jon, the new Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, promises that Dryn will serve as his own page.[1]
