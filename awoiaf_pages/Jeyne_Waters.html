Jeyne Waters was the bastard daughter of Lord Alyn Velaryon and Princess Elaena Targaryen.[2]

History

Jeyne was the bastard daughter of the seafarer Lord Alyn Velaryon, known as the Oakenfist, and Princess Elaena Targaryen. She had a twin brother, Jon Waters.[2]
