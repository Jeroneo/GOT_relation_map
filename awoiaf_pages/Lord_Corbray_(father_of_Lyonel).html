Lord Corbray was the Lord of Heart's Home and the head of House Corbray during Robert's Rebellion.

Character

He was an old man by the time of Robert's Rebellion. He wielded the ancestral Valyrian steel longsword of his house, Lady Forlorn.[1]

History

Lord Corbray fought for Lord Robert Baratheon during the Battle of the Trident. He was wounded during the battle and his eldest son and heir, Lyonel, carried him back to the maesters in the rear, while his second son, Ser Lyn, grabbed his sword, Lady Forlorn, and led a charge against the Dornishmen led by Prince Lewyn Martell.[1]

Lord Corbray was eventually succeeded by Lyonel, but gave Lady Forlorn to Lyn.[1]
