Marsella Waynwood was a noblewoman of House Waynwood. Her relation to Anya Waynwood, the Lady of Ironoaks, is unknown. She was the third wife of Ser Stevron Frey, and had one son by him, Walton Frey.[2]

History

Marsella died in childbed.[2]
