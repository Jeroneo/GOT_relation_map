Book Fire & Blood (mentioned)

Moriah Qorgyle was a noblewoman from House Qorgyle during the reign of King Aegon III Targaryen.[2]

History

After the death of Queen Jaehaera Targaryen in 133 AC, Moriah was presented to King Aegon III as a potential bride at the Maiden's Day Ball.[2]

According to Archmaester Gyldayn, she had the boldest tongue present, as she requested that the king come down from the Iron Throne and kiss her. Aegon did not oblige her request.[2]
