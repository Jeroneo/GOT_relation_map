Marselen is a Naathi member of the Unsullied. His living siblings include Mossador and Missandei.

History

Missandei and her three brothers, including Marselen and Mossador, were captured on their native island of Naath by raiders from the Basilisk Isles, and sold into slavery in Astapor. Missandei was trained as a scribe, but Marselen and his two brothers were gelded and trained by the Good Masters to be Unsullied.[2] Marselen and Mossador became Unsullied,[3] and the third brother died at some point.[4]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Daenerys Targaryen purchases Unsullied from the Good Masters of Astapor. After freeing the soldiers, she turns them against the slavers with the fall of Astapor.[2] Daenerys also takes into her service Missandei, Marselen's sister, as a scribe and handmaid.[2]

A Dance with Dragons

As Daenerys told the Unsullied to abolish the practice of choosing a new name every day,[5] Marselen chose to go back to his birth name

Mossador, a brother of Marselen and Missandei, is killed in Meereen by the Sons of the Harpy.[3] Marselen remains Missandei's only living brother.[4]

When three free companies are formed of freedmen, Marselen helps train the Mother's Men and is named their captain.[4] He represents the Mother's Men when Daenerys hold a council.[4]

Marselen accompanies Daenerys when she meets Astapori refugees outside of Meereen.[6]

After Daenerys disappears from Daznak's Pit, Marselen shares Ser Barristan Selmy's caution about warring against Yunkai.[7] He attends Barristan's council.[8]
