Osric Stark was a member of House Stark who became Lord Commander of the Night's Watch around 400 BC.

History

Osric was only ten years old when he was elected Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, making him the youngest chosen for that position.[1] Maester Balder wrote The Edge of the World during Osric's rule of sixty years.[2]
