Arra Norrey was a noblewoman of House Norrey during the second century after Aegon's Conquest, and the first wife of Lord Cregan Stark of Winterfell.[3]

History

Arra was a childhood friend of Cregan Stark.[1] Shortly after Cregan overthrew his regent uncle, Bennard Stark, and took control of the north in 126 AC, she married him. In 128 AC, Arra died while giving birth[1] to their only son together, Rickon Stark.[3]
