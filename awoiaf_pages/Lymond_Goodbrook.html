Lymond Goodbrook is the head of House Goodbrook. He is a good friend of Ser Edmure Tully.

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Lord Lymond is among the nobles from the riverlands who accompany Lord Edmure Tully to his wedding at the Twins.[1]

After the Red Wedding, Lymond is among the riverlords pardoned by King Tommen I Baratheon and is subsequently welcomed back into the king's peace.[2]

A Feast for Crows

Lord Lymond is presumably one of the riverlords present at the siege of Riverrun since Ser Jaime Lannister sees the Goodbrook banner when he arrives at Riverrun.[3]
