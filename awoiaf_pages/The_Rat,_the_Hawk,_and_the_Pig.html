The Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search
Aelora Targaryen, and the Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig, by tragedy-peanut-gallery ©

The Rat, the Hawk, and the Pig are three men who are mentioned in the history of Westeros. Their real names are not known.[1][2] As their only known actions were at least thirty years apart, it may be possible that different individuals used these names at different times.[N 1]

History

Some time between 217 AC and 221 AC[3], these three men attacked Aelora Targaryen, Princess of Dragonstone, at a masked ball. She committed suicide soon afterward.[1]

In 251 AC, the trio led a rebellion that was crushed by Prince Daeron Targaryen. Daeron perished in the final battle, alongside his lover, Ser Jeremy Norridge, but the rebels were slain or hanged.[2]
