Warryn Beesbury

From A Wiki of Ice and Fire
Jump to: navigation, search
Lord
Warryn Beesbury
Title Lord of Honeyholt[1]
Allegiance House Beesbury
Culture Reach
Book A Feast for Crows (appendix)

Warryn Beesbury is the Lord of Honeyholt and the head of House Beesbury.[1]

Family

Warryn's relation to Ben Beesbury, a recent Lord of Honeyholt,[2] is not stated in the books. However, according to semi-canon sources, Warryn is Ben's nephew and successor.[3]
