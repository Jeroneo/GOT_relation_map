Character

The querulous[3] Eldon is around seventy years old.[1]

Recent Events

A Clash of Kings

At the onset of the War of the Five Kings, Ser Eldon supports Renly Baratheon.[4] After Renly's death, Lord Estermont goes over to Stannis Baratheon's side[5] and the Estermonts fight for him in the Battle of the Blackwater.[6]

A Storm of Swords

Following the defeat at the Blackwater Rush, Ser Eldon is either captured or changes sides and is among the Estermonts who swear fealty to King Joffrey I Baratheon.[7]

A Feast for Crows

Eldon is now Lord of Greenstone.[1] As her punishment by Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne, for having conspired with Princess Arianne Martell to place Princess Myrcella Baratheon on the Iron Throne, Sylva Santagar is sent to Greenstone and quickly wed to the much older Eldon.[1]

Arianne, unaware of Sylva's marriage while imprisoned at Sunspear, worries that her father Doran might force her to wed Lord Estermont.[3]

A Dance with Dragons

Ser Marq Mandrake takes the island of Estermont during the landing of the Golden Company.[8][9][10]

Family

The Appendix of A Feast for Crows states Eldon is the uncle of Stannis Baratheon, the father of Aemon Estermont, and the brother of Lomas Estermont. However, the Appendix of A Dance with Dragons states Eldon is the great-uncle of Stannis and has two sons, Aemon and Lomas. See House Estermont/Family Trees for more information about this topic.

Quotes

Cersei: How old is the blushing bride?

Qyburn: Three-and-twenty, Your Grace. Whereas Lord Estermont—

Cersei: —must be seventy. I am aware of that.[1]

—Cersei Lannister and Qyburn

Behind the Scenes

Eldon's name is once misspelled as "Elden" in A Feast for Crows.[3]
