Meredyth Crane, known as Merry Crane, is a noblewoman of House Crane.[6][7][8]

Appearance and Character

Meredyth is a boisterous plump woman.[1] She always has a funny tale to tell.[2]

Recent Events

A Storm of Swords

Meredyth is one of Margaery Tyrell's lady attendants in King's Landing.[1]

Merry dances with Jalabhar Xho during the wedding of Tyrion Lannister and Sansa Stark.[3]

A Feast for Crows

Meredyth is part of Margaery's retinue. She often goes hawking with Janna Fossoway and Queen Margaery.[4] Merry and Janna are escorted by the twins Ser Horas and Ser Hobber Redwyne during one of Margaery's rides, returning with flowers in their hair.[9]
