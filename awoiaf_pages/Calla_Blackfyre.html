Calla Blackfyre was the eldest daughter of Daemon I Blackfyre and Rohanne of Tyrosh.

History

Prior to the First Blackfyre Rebellion, Calla's father Daemon agreed to wed her to her half-uncle, Ser Aegor Rivers. However, it is unknown if and when the marriage took place.[2]
