Elaena Targaryen was a member of House Targaryen during the Century of Blood, the eldest daughter of Lord Gaemon and Lady Daenys Targaryen of Dragonstone.

History

Elaena married her brother, Aegon, and together they had two sons: Maegon and Aerys Targaryen.[3] Elaena and Aegon also had a younger sister who married a petty lord.[4]

Elaena and Aegon inherited the lordship of Dragonstone from their father and mother, they jointly ruled Dragonstone and were succeeded by Maegon.[3]
