Rickard Ryswell is a son of Lord Rodrik Ryswell and a member of House Ryswell.[1]

Appearance and Character

Rickard's look resembles that of his brother Roger, to the point Theon Greyjoy struggles to tell them apart.[2]

Rickard and his brothers Roger and Roose are said to be quarrelsome. They each take a horsehead of a different color for their sigil, the jape being that the Ryswells cannot even agree upon the color of their arms.[3]

Recent Events

A Dance with Dragons

Theon Greyjoy sees a Ryswell lead a column of riders to the camp of Ramsay Bolton at the siege of Moat Cailin, but is unable to tell the difference between Rickard and his brother Roger.[2]

Along with the rest of House Ryswell, Rickard attends the wedding of Ramsay to "Arya Stark"—actually Jeyne Poole—in Winterfell. Theon spots Rickard nuzzling at the neck of one of Abel's washerwomen—supposedly Frenya—near the Burned Tower of Winterfell (however, as mentioned before, it is possible Theon mistakes Roger for Rickard).[4] After a foolish freerider is thrown from the Battlements Gate, Rickard laughs at his fate.[5]
